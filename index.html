Director & Choreographer

Dixon Bowles

Teacher. Choreographer. Director.
Building a new home for dance in Orlando.

I trained at Utah Valley University and got hired by Disney Jr. Live before I finished my degree. I performed with professional dance companies in New York. And then I moved to Florida and decided to build.

For nine years, I ran the competitive dance program at a major Central Florida studio as the sole choreographer, sole director, and the only person in the room every single day. I built it from 9 dancers to 23 across five competition seasons. My dancers earned titles at Groove, Inferno, Tremaine, and StarQuest. The program won ADCC Studio of Excellence. I choreographed every piece, ran every rehearsal, directed every competition weekend.

Outside the studio, I toured nationally as a Tour Director with Fly Dance Competition. I've judged competitions, taught masterclasses across the country, and choreographed for musical theatre. I know this industry from every angle as a performer, a teacher, a director, and a judge.

DWD is what I'm building now. Not inside someone else's program. Not limited by someone else's priorities. Two programs, one standard. DWD ProSeries for young competitive dancers who want real training, and DWD Collective for adults who want real choreography and real stage time. Every class designed by me. Every piece choreographed by me. That's what Dance With Dixon means.

What I Bring

Teaching Philosophy

I believe in rigor with heart. Every class should challenge you, every rehearsal should push you, and every piece of choreography should mean something. I don't do filler. I don't do lazy. But I also don't do fear-based teaching. My dancers work hard because they want to — because they feel invested, not intimidated.

Two Programs, One Standard

  • DWD ProSeries — full competitive training across all styles (jazz technique, ballet, contemporary, lyrical, hip hop, tap, acro, musical theatre) for dancers ages 5+. Three tracks. Not a studio — a dedicated training company.
  • DWD Collective — modern dance company for adults. No audition required. All levels welcome. Weekly classes and real performances.
  • Every class designed by a working director and choreographer — not pulled from a syllabus
  • Choreography that tells stories — every piece has a purpose

The Program

ProSeries is a full-spectrum competitive training program for dancers ages 5 and up. Three tracks — Prep, Elite, and Pro — each built around serious technique, artistry, and performance. This is your dancer's home for training, not an add-on.

Dancers train across all styles — jazz technique, ballet, contemporary, lyrical, hip hop, tap, acro, musical theatre — with an emphasis on jazz and ballet technique as the foundation for everything else. Every class is designed by a working choreographer and director, not pulled from a syllabus.

This isn't a recreational program and it isn't a studio. ProSeries is a company — a dedicated training environment built to develop well-rounded, professional-level dancers who are as strong in artistry as they are in technique.

Why ProSeries

I spent five years as a competition director inside the traditional studio model. I saw what worked. I saw a lot more of what didn't. ProSeries is what I'd build if I could start from zero — so that's exactly what I'm doing.

Not a Studio

Traditional studios make their money from recreational classes and little kids. The competitive team is an afterthought — a revenue add-on, not the mission. ProSeries flips that. Competitive training is the mission. Every hour, every dollar, every decision is pointed at developing serious dancers.

Built by a Director

Every class, every rehearsal, every piece of choreography — that's me in the room. Not a guest teacher, not a rotating staff. I built this program and I'm the one running it. That's what "Dance With Dixon" means.

Full Training in All Styles

Jazz technique. Ballet. Contemporary. Lyrical. Hip hop. Tap. Acro. Musical theatre. Your dancer won't specialize too early or miss entire genres. The emphasis is on jazz and ballet technique — the foundation that makes everything else possible — with real depth across every style.

Artists, Not Just Competitors

Trophies are great. I've won plenty. But I'm not training your kid to collect hardware — I'm training them to walk into any room and be the most prepared, most expressive, most professional dancer there. The trophies follow.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

What We Collect

When you submit a form on this site (interest forms, contact forms, email signups), we collect the information you provide — name, email, phone number, and any details about your dancer. We do not collect data passively or use tracking cookies.

How We Use It

Your information is used solely to communicate with you about DWD programs, events, and registration. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Where It's Stored

Form submissions are stored securely via Supabase, a cloud database provider with industry-standard encryption. Only DWD staff have access to submitted data.

Children's Information

ProSeries serves dancers ages 5 and up. We collect children's names and ages only through parent-submitted forms. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children.

Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete your information at any time by emailing dancewithdixon@gmail.com.
